### What is Vidcast?


Vidcast is a video messaging tool integrated with Webex that allows users to create and share video messages to enhance communication. Users can record video messages, add comments, and share them directly in Webex spaces or groups. It supports features like AI-generated highlights, smart chapters, and AI assistant capabilities to improve video content management and engagement.

### Key Features and Management Capabilities:


Access and Permissions: Administrators can enable or disable Vidcast access, recording, and commenting for users in the organization via Control Hub.

Integration: Vidcast integrates with Webex and Microsoft Teams, allowing users to import meeting recordings and access Vidcast features within these platforms.

AI Features: Includes AI-generated highlights, chapters, and an AI assistant for generative tasks. Enterprise customers can use AI to generate Vidcasts from PowerPoint or PDF files automatically.

Data Management: Controls for video downloads, external sharing, and uploads help maintain security and compliance.

Sharing Permissions: Default sharing settings can be configured as restricted, private, or open link, with options to restrict sharing to specific domains.

Video Retention Policies: Administrators can set retention periods for Vidcast videos, from default durations to custom periods between 7 and 3600 days.

Classification Tags: Videos can be tagged for easier organization and compliance, with options to require classification before saving.

Language Settings: Translated captions and transcripts can be enabled for accessibility in multiple languages.

Company Homepage: Organizations can create a dedicated Vidcast homepage to showcase curated video content relevant to their users.

### Using Vidcast in Webex App:


Users can record Vidcasts directly in Webex Messaging by selecting a person or space and clicking "Record a Vidcast."

After recording, users can add a text message and post the video to the space.

Screen sharing capabilities require downloading the Vidcast desktop app.

### Recent Enhancements:


Automatic generation of highlights and chapters for easier video navigation.

Playlists (formerly collections) for organizing videos with sequential playback.

Enhanced sharing options to share videos within Webex spaces with controlled access.

A new Vidcast desktop app to assist with recording.

This comprehensive management and integration make Vidcast a powerful tool for video communication and collaboration within Cisco Webex environments 1, 2, 3.
